Answers to frequently asked questions:

How can the IT Recovery Specialists benefit our organization?

The employees at IT Recovery Specialists specialize in converting surplus assets into cash or new equipment. We can also provide low cost transportation and storage, positively impacting your bottom line.

What types of equipment do you accept, and in what condition?

We accept all types of equipment in any condition. If remarketing isn't feasible, we may advise you of other disposal options, such as scrapping.

Who pays for the transportation of my surplus equipment?

IT Recovery Specialists normally pays for the transportation on your surplus equipment and deducts the cost from the sale proceeds.

How much do IT Recovery Specialists' remarketing services cost and will we be billed?

Our fee's are deducted from the proceeds of the sale. Therefore no invoice will be issued.

Who normally purchases surplus equipment from IT Recovery Specialists?

We normally try to sell surplus equipment from one business unit which no longer has a use for it to one that does. We're also connected with hundreds of wholesale brokers and dealers throughout the U.S.

How do I get Started?

Just contact us and we'll get you started. Or you may want to review our Services or Products first.
